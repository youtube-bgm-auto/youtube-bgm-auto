Privacy Policy

SleepScape — Last updated: May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how SleepScape ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our Pinterest content automation application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application accesses the Pinterest account of the account owner solely to create and publish Pins on their behalf. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from Pinterest users or third parties.

2. How We Use Information

The Pinterest API access token is stored locally on the operator's own device and is used exclusively to post Pins to the SleepScape Pinterest account. No data is transmitted to any third-party server operated by us.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to outside parties. Our application does not collect data from Pinterest users other than the account owner.
